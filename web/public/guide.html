Food Redistribution Guide

Step-by-step instructions for safely sharing food with your community

For Food Donors

1

Prepare Your Food

Ensure food is fresh, properly stored, and within safe consumption dates. Package items securely and label with preparation date if applicable.

2

Post on BloomNet

Use our app to create a donation listing. Include food type, quantity, location, and available pickup times. Be specific about any dietary restrictions or special handling requirements.

3

Coordinate Pickup

Respond to requests promptly. Confirm pickup time and location. Have food ready and easily accessible for the recipient.

4

Complete the Exchange

Meet the recipient at the agreed time. Verify the pickup in the app. Thank them for helping reduce food waste!

For Food Recipients

1

Browse Available Food

Use the map view to find food donations near you. Filter by food type, distance, and quantity to find the best matches.

2

Request Food

Contact the donor through the platform. Confirm quantity needed and discuss pickup arrangements. Be respectful of the donor's time and location.

3

Pick Up Food

Arrive on time at the agreed location. Bring appropriate containers if needed. Inspect food for quality and safety before accepting.

4

Distribute & Enjoy

Distribute food to those in need promptly. Store properly if not using immediately. Mark the exchange as complete in the app.

Safety Guidelines

✅

Food Safety: Only share food that is fresh, properly stored, and within safe consumption dates. When in doubt, don't share.

✅

Hygiene: Wash hands before handling food. Use clean containers and packaging materials.

✅

Temperature: Keep hot food hot and cold food cold. Use insulated containers for transport when necessary.

✅

Allergens: Always label food with potential allergens. Recipients should ask about ingredients if unsure.

✅

Communication: Be clear about food condition, preparation methods, and any special requirements.

Best Practices

For Donors

  • • Post food as soon as you know it's available
  • • Be honest about food condition
  • • Respond to requests within 24 hours
  • • Be flexible with pickup times
  • • Consider donating regularly

For Recipients

  • • Only request what you can use
  • • Arrive on time for pickups
  • • Communicate clearly about needs
  • • Show appreciation to donors
  • • Report any issues promptly
