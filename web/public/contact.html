Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Simply create an account and start posting food donations or requests. Our matching system will connect you with nearby users.

Is there a fee to use BloomNet?

No, BloomNet is completely free to use. We're committed to making food sharing accessible to everyone.

How do I ensure food safety?

Check our comprehensive guide for food safety guidelines. Always inspect food before accepting and follow proper storage procedures.

Can businesses participate?

Absolutely! Restaurants, cafes, and food businesses are welcome to donate surplus food through our platform.
