Our Mission

Hunger and waste coexist in our communities. We bridge this gap by creating a seamless connection between food donors, NGOs, and local shelters using geolocation-based coordination.

Together, we can ensure that surplus food reaches those who need it most, reducing waste while fighting hunger in our communities.
