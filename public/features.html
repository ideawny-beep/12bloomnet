Platform Features

🗺️

Live Map

Real-time visualization of all food donations and requests on an interactive map. See exactly where food is available and where it's needed most.

🎯

Smart Matching

Automated geospatial matching connects donors with nearby shelters based on location, ensuring quick and efficient food redistribution.

📍

Geolocation

Automatic location detection using browser geolocation API and Nominatim geocoding for accurate positioning and distance calculations.

📅

Pickup Scheduling

Coordinate pickup times efficiently with integrated scheduling and queue management. Track pickup status in real-time.

💬

Messaging System

Direct communication between donors and recipients to coordinate details, ask questions, and confirm arrangements.

📊

Analytics & Stats

Track platform impact with metrics including meals redistributed, average pickup time, repeat donors, and active users.

🔄

Real-time Updates

Live synchronization across the platform ensures you always see the latest donations and requests as they're posted.

✅

Safety Checks

Built-in verification workflows and hygiene checks to ensure food safety and quality for all recipients.
