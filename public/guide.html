How to Use BloomNet

For Food Donors

  1. Navigate to the App - Click on "App" in the navigation menu
  2. Enable Location - Allow your browser to access your location when prompted
  3. Fill Out the Donation Form - Provide:
    • Your organization or name
    • Description of the food
    • Quantity (number of meals/servings)
    • Contact phone number
    • How long the food will be available
  4. Post Your Donation - Click "Post Donation" to make it visible on the map
  5. Wait for Contact - Recipients will see your donation and contact you directly

For Food Recipients (Shelters & NGOs)

  1. Access the App - Navigate to the application page
  2. Enable Location - Share your location to see nearby donations
  3. Browse the Map - View all available donations shown as green markers
  4. Post a Request (Optional) - Fill out the request form with:
    • Your organization name
    • Type of organization
    • Number of people you need to feed
    • Contact information
  5. Contact Donors - Click on map markers to see donor details and contact them
  6. Coordinate Pickup - Arrange a convenient pickup time with the donor

Tips for Success

  • Always enable location services for accurate matching
  • Provide accurate contact information
  • Be responsive when contacted
  • Coordinate pickup times clearly
  • Follow food safety guidelines
  • Update the status of your donation/request when completed
  • Check the map regularly for new opportunities
