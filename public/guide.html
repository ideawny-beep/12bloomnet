How to Use BloomNet

A simple guide to get you started — whether you're donating or requesting food.

🍽️ For Food Donors

1

Navigate to the App

Click on "Launch App" in the navigation menu to open the food sharing dashboard.

2

Enable Location

Allow your browser to access your location when prompted. This helps us match you with nearby recipients.

3

Fill Out the Donation Form

Provide the following details:

  • Your organization or name
  • Description of the food
  • Quantity (number of meals/servings)
  • Contact phone number
  • How long the food will be available
4

Post Your Donation

Click "Post Donation" to make it visible on the map for nearby recipients.

5

Wait for Contact

Recipients will see your donation on the live map and contact you directly to arrange pickup.

🏠 For Food Recipients

1

Access the App

Navigate to the application page from the main menu.

2

Enable Location

Share your location to see nearby donations on the map.

3

Browse the Map

View all available donations shown as green markers on the interactive map.

4

Post a Request (Optional)

Fill out the request form with your organization name, type, number of people, and contact info.

5

Contact Donors

Click on map markers to see donor details and reach out directly.

6

Coordinate Pickup

Arrange a convenient pickup time with the donor via phone.

💡 Tips for Success

  • Always enable location services for accurate matching
  • Provide accurate contact information
  • Be responsive when contacted
  • Coordinate pickup times clearly
  • Follow food safety guidelines
  • Update the status of your donation/request when completed
  • Check the map regularly for new opportunities
