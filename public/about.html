About BloomNet

Building a world where no food goes to waste while anyone goes hungry.

🎯

Our Mission

BloomNet is dedicated to fighting hunger and reducing food waste by connecting food donors with organizations and shelters that serve those in need. We believe that no food should go to waste while people go hungry.

💡

Our Vision

Through innovative geolocation technology and smart matching algorithms, we make it easy for restaurants, grocery stores, and individuals to share surplus food with local NGOs, shelters, and community organizations.

How We Help

Bridging the gap between surplus and need

For Donors

  • Easy posting of surplus food
  • Automatic matching with nearby recipients
  • Flexible pickup scheduling
  • Track your impact and contributions

For Recipients

  • Access to local food donations
  • Real-time notifications of nearby food
  • Direct communication with donors
  • Coordinate pickups efficiently
🌍

Our Impact

Since our launch, BloomNet has facilitated thousands of food donations, helping to feed communities while reducing food waste. Every donation makes a difference in someone's life.

Join us in building a world where surplus food reaches those who need it most, creating stronger, more sustainable communities.
